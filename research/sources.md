# Research Sources: AI-Powered SEO Content Production for B2B SaaS

## Research Topic

AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS.

## Project Thesis

B2B SaaS SEO is shifting from keyword-only blog production to AI-visible content systems built around expert insight, audience research, topical authority, content distribution, and measurement beyond traffic.

## Why I Chose This Topic

I chose this topic because B2B SaaS companies are facing a major shift in how buyers discover, evaluate, and trust content.

SEO is no longer only about ranking articles on Google. Buyers now discover information through Google Search, AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, LinkedIn, YouTube, newsletters, communities, podcasts, review sites, and third-party mentions.

This research project studies how strong SEO and content practitioners are adapting to this shift.

## Research Focus

This project focuses on:

* AI-assisted content production
* SEO strategy for B2B SaaS
* AI search visibility
* Zero-click content
* Audience research
* Content quality
* E-E-A-T and expert authority
* Topical authority
* Content distribution
* Measurement beyond traffic

## Expert Selection Criteria

Experts were selected based on:

* Direct experience in SEO, content marketing, organic growth, or B2B SaaS growth
* Practical content from LinkedIn, YouTube, podcasts, blogs, webinars, or newsletters
* Clear thinking around AI search, content production, SEO strategy, or audience research
* Ability to support a future playbook, not just provide generic opinions
* Evidence that they actually practice what they teach

# Source Quality Scorecard

## Purpose

This scorecard evaluates whether the selected experts are strong enough to support a real B2B SaaS AI-powered SEO content production playbook.

The goal is not to collect random marketing opinions. The goal is to identify practitioners whose content shows experience, strategic thinking, technical awareness, and practical usefulness.

## Scoring Criteria

Each expert is scored from 1 to 5 across five areas:

| Criteria                         | Meaning                                                                                       |
| -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Practitioner Credibility         | Does this person actually work in SEO, content, organic growth, or B2B SaaS growth?           |
| B2B SaaS Relevance               | Is their thinking useful for B2B SaaS companies?                                              |
| AI Search / AI Content Relevance | Do they discuss AI search, AI-assisted content, AEO, GEO, AI Overviews, or the future of SEO? |
| Tactical Usefulness              | Can their ideas become practical steps in a future playbook?                                  |
| Recency                          | Is their content recent enough to reflect the current AI-search shift?                        |

The selected experts are not general marketing influencers. They were chosen because they represent different parts of the AI-powered SEO content production system:

* AI search and answer engine visibility
